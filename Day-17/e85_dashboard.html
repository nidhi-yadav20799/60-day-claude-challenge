Fuel Type Comparison

Petrol (E20)

  • Wide refuel availability, 5 min average fill time
  • Best resale value and mechanic familiarity
  • Highest fuel price per unit (₹100)
  • Highest average cost/km among liquid fuels
Best for: Mixed city/highway driving, your current setup

Diesel

  • Strong mileage on long highway stretches
  • Lower fuel price/unit than petrol (₹91)
  • Highest maintenance cost/km (₹1.00)
  • Higher CO₂/km than most alternatives
Best for: High-distance highway commuters

CNG

  • Cheapest running cost among combustion fuels (₹3.32/km)
  • Good mileage, 24.1 km/kg average
  • Limited fuel station network
  • Boot space reduced by cylinder
Best for: Budget-conscious daily city driving

Electric (EV)

  • Lowest cost/km overall (₹1.75/km)
  • Lowest maintenance/km (₹0.23)
  • Longest recharge time, 45 min average
  • Needs charging infrastructure access
Best for: Urban commutes with home/office charging

E85 (Flex-Fuel)

  • Lowest CO₂/km of all fuels (0.070 kg/km)
  • Lowest maintenance/km among combustion fuels (₹0.46)
  • Highest cost/km in dataset (₹6.37/km)
  • Lowest mileage, 12.9 km/l average
Best for: Emission-conscious drivers open to a running-cost premium
