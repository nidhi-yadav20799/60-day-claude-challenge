# Key Learnings

* Chain-of-Thought prompting generated a structured and personalized career roadmap instead of a generic response.
* Asking targeted questions before generating the roadmap helped Claude understand my current situation and career goals.
* The roadmap clearly identified my strengths, skill gaps, learning priorities, suggested projects, and networking strategy.
* Breaking the problem into logical reasoning steps produced more actionable recommendations.
* Personalized prompts result in higher-quality outputs that are more relevant to real career planning.
